Frequently Asked Questions

How do i apply to join ipNX?

If you wish to send an application to us,
please look for a vacancy in our Job Search Engine.
The method of application will be clearly stated
in the job description.

Do you accept applications in hardcopy?

No, you'll need to apply online. This makes it easier, for the application form to be printed and saved, so you can log back in to complete the remaining sections.

Is there an age limit for applicants applying for a graduate job?

No. we are an equal - opportunities employer. We don't discriminate based on age.
