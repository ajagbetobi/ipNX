FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Looking for a quick overview of the opportunities available with ipNX? Or is there something you're unsure about? You should find the information here.

How do I apply to join ipNX?

If you wish to send an application to us,
please look for a vacancy in our Job Search Engine.
The method of application will be clearly stated
in the job description.

Do you accept applications in hardcopy?

No, you'll need to apply online. This makes it easier, for the application form to be printed and saved, so you can log back in to complete the remaining sections.

Is there an age limit for applicants applying for a graduate job?

No. we are an equal - opportunities employer. We don't discriminate based on age.

I can’t find a suitable job on your website, can I still apply?

We accept speculative applications, CVs or resumes via email. You can also apply to a particular vacancy found through our Job Search engine. If you sign up for our Job Alert service, we’ll email you with notifications of new vacancies matching your preferred criteria when they become available.

If I submit an application or register my details, will ipNX keep me on file?

All candidates details are stored on our central database for three years, unless we receive a request by an individual to delete their personal record. These records enable us to search against candidate profiles with specific skill sets, should we identify specific recruitment needs in the future.

What qualification do I need to work at ipNX?

Generally, a candidate is expected to hold a degree to be considered for any of our vacancies.

How long after applying can I expect to wait before hearing anything?

You will receive an acknowledgement within 48 hours to confirm we have your application.

What do you mean by ‘Professional’?

Our professional opportunities are for experienced people with varying skill-sets and expertise.

Do I require a Telecommunications industry experience to join ipNX?

It depends on the position you’re interested in. Many technical roles require you to have spent time in a similar job and environment, while others require less specific experience. Some of our job opportunities are based on generic business skill-sets and thus may not require experience of the telecom industry.
